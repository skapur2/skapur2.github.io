PERK KAFE

162 E 37th St New York, NY 10016


Perk Kafe is a lovely spot to settle down for the day and get work done. Small business with a big personality. Perk Kafe is an awesome cafe with a lot of character, delivering perfect coffee and free wifi in the process.

The brick walls give this spot the most homey vibe. The baristas are attentive and friendly. The menu is simple and to the point. The espresso drinks are broken down by ounce size and there is a great selection of seasonal teas and tea lattes.

Once you cozy up at Perk Kafe you will not want to leave. This is a personal favorite of mine not too far from work. My friend and I find ourselves taking the neverending coffee break here pretty frequently.
